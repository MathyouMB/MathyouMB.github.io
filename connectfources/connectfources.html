Connect Fources is a modded game of Connect Four where you can choose to change the direction of gravity instead of placing a disk. Disks also break over time, and when broken the disks above the broken disk will fall in the direction of the new gravity. The game was orignally programmed in JavaFx for a highschool summative project, however I'm now looking to re-create the game in a language capable of doing online multiplayer. I'm also working on a single player AI to play against you. The current demo build is available via my github.
